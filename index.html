About Me

Welcome! I am a linguist, ESL instructor, and visual artist. My academic research focuses heavily on sociolinguistics, computational corpus analysis, and digital ethnography, exploring how marginalized communities navigate identity through creative language practices.

Alongside my research, my art serves as a parallel avenue for expression, exploring themes of structure, mosaic, and identity. Use the tabs above to explore my academic portfolio or view my creative gallery.
